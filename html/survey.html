Internet Addiction Survey

*Note this survey is not 100% accurate, this is to test your self-awareness and control of your electronic devices.
This is a 10 question survey, and every question is required to be filled in to sumbit
Question 1: Do you find yourself frustrated and/or upset when others seek your attention while you are online?





Question 2: I use the internet as a escape from my life.





Question 3: I have tried to reduce my smartphone or internet use without success.





Question 4: Have you tried to spend less time using your internet-connected device (desktop, smartphone, or tablet) but found it challenging?





Question 5: I feel anxious when I do not have my smartphone with me.





Question 6: Are you trying to fall asleep while browsing the internet but having trouble doing so and/or are you waking frequently to check your internet-connected device?





Question 7: When using the internet do you find that you lose track of time and feel surprised when you realize the time spent surfing the web?





Question 8: Have you been ashamed of or tried to hide how much time you spend on your internet-connected device?





Question 9: I’d rather stay home gaming, doing social media, or otherwise spending time online than go out with my friends.





Question 10: Do you find yourself using the internet in specific situations where it might be less appropriate? For example, at school during class, while at work despite rules against it, during a ceremony (funeral/wedding/service), at a theater during a concert/play/movie, etc.
