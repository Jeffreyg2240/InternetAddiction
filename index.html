Reduce Internet Addiction

To reduce the compulsive urge for internet usage through spreading self-awareness.

Mission

To spread awareness of compulsive internet usage, also known as internet addiction, and of the organizations that cope with it. Many people think they know what it is but aren’t aware of the symptoms, even minor symptoms can lead to a major project. Furthermore, compulsive internet usage has a correlation with other mental illness, like depression, anxiety, and low self-esteem.
