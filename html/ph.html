For iOS users:

Screen Time

The Screen Time app is built into your iPhones on devices with iOS 12 or newer. It has the functionality to view and limit your phone you or your children's phone time.

How to get to Screen Time:

Settings > ScreenTime
Click on downtime to enable it and set a time/app limit on App Limit, Always Allowed, and Content & Privacy Restrictions.
Furthermore you can set a passcode and set up the same access for other devices to easy access.
